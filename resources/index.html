Resources

Here is some media I’ve found to be very useful in my journey of trying to break into the profession of privacy. They range from legal discussions to career advice to improving your own personal privacy.

Videos¶

  • What is a privacy engineer? – an interview with Ben Kaplan, a privacy engineer at Google who explains the role and also discusses his time at OneTrust during the introduction of the GDPR
  • How to break down barriers to privacy careers for underrepresented populations – a University of California webinar featuring university CISOs, Chief Privacy Officers, et al., discussing their unique and nascent positions in privacy in the education sector, and overcoming diversity challenges in the workforce
  • Privacy Engineering 101 – an interview with Erin Relford, another Google privacy engineer who describes a day in the life at work, from translating requirements between technical and non-technical departments and other important concepts to know
  • (AWS re:Invent 2022) AWS and privacy engineering – a technical session that describes the realized benefits of privacy, and how various AWS privacy-services can simplify problems like in-line data ingestion and transformation, PI redaction, and consent management
  • (OneTrust) How to grow your privacy career – a OneTrust webinar featuring its CEO and the privacy leads of large companies like HP, LiveNation, and Grindr, who discuss privacy challenges they’ve overcome, the lessons they’ve learned, and career advice
  • The beauty of (big) data privacy engineering – a technical presentation on how data-heavy company Truata achieved performant anonymization and privacy-preserving analytics at scale

Channels/Companies¶

  • Privado – uploads its technical product demos of static-code scanning to discover privacy concerns and violations, and also interviews with DPOs, privacy engineers, and privacy counsels
    • My favorites:
      • Privacy by code: filling gaps in your privacy program
      • Privacy star insights: Aleatha Parker-Wood
  • Transcend – similar to WireWheel, posts conversations on various privacy topics.
    • My favorites:
      • Defining and defending a privacy budget for 2023
      • Preparing for employee DSAR under CPRA
  • WireWheel.io – hosts webinars with bright minds in the industry on hot topics and pressing issues
    • My favorites:
      • Adveritising leaders roundtable
      • Innovating DSAR fulfillment with Microsoft

Podcasts¶

  • Data Protection Breakfast Club – “a legal podcast that doesn’t suck”, featuring conversations with various GC’s (general counsels), privacy and policy leaders
  • Privacy Pros – a career-focused podcast featuring privacy and legal professionals discussing how to break into and specialize in the privacy field
  • Surveillance Report – a weekly privacy and infosec podcast that covers breaking news stories on data breaches, companies, research, and politics, with a focus on how to secure and mitigate your digital footprint
