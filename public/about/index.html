About this site¶

This site is statically generated at runtime via Hugo, meaning what you see is exactly what you get. There are no cookies, no trackers, and no nonsense to worry about.

This site also implements privacy-respecting YouTube embeds, meaning you won’t load any content from YouTube’s servers until you decide to click on a video, as thumbnails and preview information are hosted by the site. Thanks to Joost van Der Schee.
