Here is some media I've found to be very useful in my journey of trying to break into the profession of privacy. They range from legal discussions to career advice to improving your own personal privacy.

## Videos
* [**`What is a privacy engineer?`**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-GFQlDB5i5I) -- an interview with Ben Kaplan, a privacy engineer at Google who explains the role and also discusses his time at OneTrust during the introduction of the GDPR
* [**`How to break down barriers to privacy careers for underrepresented populations`**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=crLvwBnTGQI) -- a University of California webinar featuring university CISOs, Chief Privacy Officers, et al., discussing their unique and nascent positions in privacy in the education sector, and overcoming diversity challenges in the workforce
* [**`Privacy Engineering 101`**](https://youtu.be/H6wFYBF0Uvk?t=645) -- an interview with Erin Relford, another Google privacy engineer who describes a day in the life at work, from translating requirements between technical and non-technical departments and other important concepts to know
* [**`(AWS re:Invent 2022) AWS and privacy engineering`**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Iy8ivHE0Ko) -- a technical session that describes the realized benefits of privacy, and how various AWS privacy-services can simplify problems like in-line data ingestion and transformation, PI redaction, and consent management
* [**`(OneTrust) How to grow your privacy career`**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qD3_8z--Xfg) -- a OneTrust webinar featuring its CEO and the privacy leads of large companies like HP, LiveNation, and Grindr, who discuss privacy challenges they've overcome, the lessons they've learned, and career advice
* [**`The beauty of (big) data privacy engineering`**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mrDi70FmCgk) -- a technical presentation on how data-heavy company [Truata](https://www.truata.com/) achieved performant anonymization and privacy-preserving analytics at scale


## Channels/Companies

		* [`Privacy by code: filling gaps in your privacy program`](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p3-mg3h-C0M)

## Podcasts
* [**`Data Protection Breakfast Club`**](https://open.spotify.com/show/1gaFsMrqj7UjmJHnRAABMC) -- "a legal podcast that doesn't suck", featuring conversations with various GC's (general counsels), privacy and policy leaders
* [**`Privacy Pros`**](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRGvT8aMrZCmU_ASWqk8h5KdG9OVdTZe4) -- a podcast focused on career-growth featuring privacy and legal professionals discussing how to break into and specialize in the privacy field
* [**`Surveillance Report`**](https://www.youtube.com/c/SurveillanceReport) -- a weekly privacy and infosec podcast that covers breaking news stories on data breaches, companies, research, and politics; also explains how to mitigate your [digital footprint](https://builtin.com/cybersecurity/digital-footprint)
